// process

From idea to architecture
in six steps

No cloud expertise needed. Just describe what you want to build — our AI does the rest.

STEP 01
💬

Describe Your Idea

Type your requirement in plain English. "Build a real-time analytics platform for 10M events/day" — that's all we need.

STEP 02
🧠

AI Reasons Like an Expert

Amazon Bedrock analyzes your input, selects the right services, and maps out connections using deep architectural knowledge.

STEP 03
🏗️

Architecture Blueprint

Get a complete architecture plan with service list, why each service was chosen, and how everything integrates.

STEP 04
💰

Live Cost Estimate

Real-time pricing pulled from the AWS Pricing API. Know your monthly bill before writing a single line of code.

STEP 05
📊

Visual Diagram

Auto-generated architecture diagrams using Mermaid.js — interactive, shareable, and ready for your presentation.

STEP 06
🔍

Audit & Improve

Paste your existing architecture and get instant feedback — find bottlenecks, cut costs, and compare alternatives.
